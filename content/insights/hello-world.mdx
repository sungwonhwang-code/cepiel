I've been meaning to start writing publicly for a while. The problem with research blogging
isn't lack of things to say — it's the gap between what you can say in a paper and what's
actually useful to someone trying to build something today.

This site is where I'll try to close that gap. Topics I expect to come back to:

- **Digital twins for chemical processes** — not the marketing kind, the kind that actually
  predicts a real plant well enough to be useful.
- **AI in chemical engineering** — what's working, what's hype, and where the interesting
  open problems are.
- **Building CEPiEL** — notes from running a deeptech company out of a research lab.

If any of that sounds interesting, the [Insights](/insights) page is the index, and you
can [reach me here](/contact).

— Sean
